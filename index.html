Our Story

Wok N’ Roll is the place to get to! It’s our home, our love, and our pride and joy.
It’s an undeniably exotic beauty. It’s a blend of cuisines from around the world.
Its colours, stories, customs, sounds, and flavours form a brilliant, heady mix that
is impossible to ignore or forget. We’re right in the middle of the capital, Port Louis.
But, our restaurant does things differently. You’ll be thinking, moving, and eating
purer than pure with Wok N’ Roll. Our farm supplies our restaurant with local produce.
Wok N’ Roll offers unique culinary experiences for foodies willing to experience
the flavours we have to offer. We make food to travel for. It’s all about the people
and places that make the island tick. This is your chance to restore your equilibrium
throughout. Just everything you need. This is where you bring friends, business
colleagues to celebrate life…


Farming Forever

Our pesticide-free farm is our own source of ingredients. We grow our own fruit and
vegetables without using soil. Instead, we go hydroponic, as plants absorb nutrients
faster. It’s clean, effective, sustainable, and incredible. In one of the greenhouses,
our rustic, vegetarian restaurant connects you to the agricultural context. You’re
seeing what you’re eating at all stages of its life. And our farm workshops share the
secrets of making delicious plant-based dishes. And beautiful green juices. You’ll be
thinking, moving, and eating purer than pure with Wok N’ Roll.
